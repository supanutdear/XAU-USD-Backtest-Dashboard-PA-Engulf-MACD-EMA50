Entry

BUY Signal Long

  • Bullish candle (Close > Open)
  • Lower wick grabs the low of previous 2 bars
  • Close above the high of previous 2 bars (Engulf)
  • MACD Line > Signal Line (Bullish momentum)
  • Price closing above EMA50 (Uptrend)

SELL Signal Short

  • Bearish candle (Close < Open)
  • Upper wick grabs the high of previous 2 bars
  • Close below the low of previous 2 bars (Engulf)
  • MACD Line < Signal Line (Bearish momentum)
  • Price closing below EMA50 (Downtrend)

Stop Loss & Take Profit

  • Entry: Close price of signal bar
  • Stop Loss: Bar Low (BUY) / Bar High (SELL)
  • Take Profit: Entry ± (SL Distance × RR Ratio)
  • Skip: If SL Distance > 0.4% of price

Money Management

  • Risk per trade: 3% of current capital (compound)
  • RR Ratio: 1:1 (TP = SL distance)
  • No overlapping trades — wait for current trade to close
  • Skip new signals while a trade is active
